# Changelog

## v1.0 — 2026

First public release. A complete English translation built on jjjewel's Rev 0.80 patch.

### Added
- ~840 newly translated strings covering the previously-Japanese remainder: Mii News
  articles, quizzes, song titles / music UI, and dialogue.
- Romaji labels on the name-index jump buttons (a, ka, sa, ta, na, ha, ma, ya, ra, wa).
- English banner title shown in the DS/DSi system menu.
- All 26,405 message entries across 22 archives audited (0 drift, byte-verified).

### Included from jjjewel Rev 0.80 (unchanged)
- Menus, Mii creation, shop, fonts, and redrawn graphics.

### Left in Japanese by design
- Kana keyboard pages (English QWERTY "A" tab provided and remembered).
- Voice-synthesizer text and song lyrics (that text is the audio).
- A few decorative logo/sign graphics (each carries an English caption).

### Known
- ~200 auto-assembled sentence fragments may read slightly stiff in some combinations.
  Reports with better phrasings are welcome via Issues.
