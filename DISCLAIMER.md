# Disclaimer & terms of use

This is an unofficial, **non-commercial fan translation** of *Tomodachi Collection*.

## What this project is

- A **patch** — a binary diff (`.xdelta`) that adds an English translation to the game.
- The patch contains the translation work (text, repointed tables, and jjjewel's
  redrawn graphics), **not** Nintendo's game. It is useless on its own; it only does
  anything when applied to a copy of the game you already own.

## What this project is not

- **It is not a ROM, and no ROM is distributed here.** You must dump your own copy of a
  cartridge you legally own. Do not ask for ROM links; requests will be closed.
- **It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Nintendo** in any way.
- **It is not for sale.** Do not sell this patch, bundle it with a ROM for sale, or
  distribute patched/playable ROMs. If you mirror or share it, share the patch only, with
  credit, and keep it free.
